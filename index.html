I'm Ansh Ruhela, BS19, and I hope to be your Institute Sports Secretary this year. My campaign runs on three points: uplifting everyone with an inclusive sporting community, shaping the leaders of tomorrow, and winning back what is our due. All buildings need a good foundation.

Over the past few years, I've seen the building blocks of this foundation slip from our hands. Inter-IIT is fast approaching, and we need a strong organisational hand to lead us to our twenty third General Championship victory. We need experienced leaders to revive the vibrant culture that first brought people like me to sports. Let me prove to you, that I am that hand.
